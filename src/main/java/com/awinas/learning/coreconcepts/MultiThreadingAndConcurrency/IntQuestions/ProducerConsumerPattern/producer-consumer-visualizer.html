💡 What's Happening Right Now

Click Step to go one step at a time, or Auto Play to watch the full animation. The Producer (Kitchen) creates dishes and puts them in the buffer. The Consumer (Waiter) takes dishes from the buffer. When the buffer is full, Producer calls wait() and goes to sleep. When the buffer is empty, Consumer calls wait() and goes to sleep. After producing/consuming, they call notifyAll() to wake the other.
